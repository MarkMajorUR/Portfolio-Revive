A Little About Me

Welcome fellow Humans

Neighbors in Need

Neighbors is Need allows anyone to become a community organizer. Concerned citizens post events such as a free dinner, or handing out winter coats. These events are then marked on the map with a pin. Neighbors in Need can click on a pin and the time, place, and nature of the event appear.
